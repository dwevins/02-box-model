Takashi Murakami

Murakami is a Japanese artist. He invented the Superflat style out of frustration with contemporary Japanese art.

Superflat

Superflat is a postmodern, manga-based form that combines cheerful colors with often disturbing or grotesque designs.

Use

Luxury fashion company Louis Vuitton launched a collabortion with Murakami to produce superflat designs for their bags.
